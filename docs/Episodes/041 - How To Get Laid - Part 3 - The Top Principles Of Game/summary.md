## Core Concepts

**Hook Point**: The moment when a woman clearly signals sexual attraction through attention shift, physical receptiveness, asking questions, qualifying herself, giving compliments, laughing at non-funny things, difficulty maintaining eye contact, or following your physical leads.

**Law of State Transference**: The principle that your emotional state, energy, and mood directly transfer to women through subcomputational signals (eye contact, posture, tonality, body language). Women are highly attuned to reading these signals.

**Man-to-Woman Frame**: A sexual, intimate communication style that is neither business-like, transactional, nor friendly. It involves subtle hints of attraction, physical touch, playful teasing, and a predatory (but smiling) sexual gaze.

**The Three Second Rule and Thirty Second Game**: Approach a woman within three seconds of noticing her attraction to avoid hesitation and fear. Play the 30-second game by forcing yourself to approach someone new within 30-second intervals to overcome analysis paralysis.

**Context Sensitivity and Calibration**: The requirement to adapt all techniques, lines, and escalation methods to fit the specific social environment, time of day, location type, and group dynamics present.

**In-Field is King**: Experiential learning through actual interactions vastly outweighs theory consumption. Field testing, failures, and real-world calibration build embodied mastery that theory alone cannot.

**True Value as Provider**: Becoming genuinely valuable by developing yourself across multiple dimensions—financially, physically, psychologically, lifestyle-wise, intellectually—so you provide real value to partners rather than leech from them.
