## Overview

Leo introduces 5-MeO-MALT, a novel research psychedelic he claims is as powerful or more powerful than 5-MeO-DMT. He describes its unique characteristics, dosing protocols, administration methods, and its potential for spiritual awakening and god-realization. More significantly, Leo announces a major shift in his teaching approach, emphasizing psychedelics as the primary tool for spiritual awakening rather than meditation or traditional practices.

## Key Insights

- **5-MeO-MALT characteristics:** Produces god-realization experiences similar to 5-MeO-DMT but with a different "flavor"—milder visuals with faint magenta/neon coloring, more stable and holistic consciousness expansion, and a lower/more manageable body load than 5-MeO-DMT
- **Stability and contemplative depth:** Unlike mushrooms and LSD which create loopy, tangential thinking patterns, 5-MeO-MALT allows the mind to maintain extended chains of thought and penetrate deeply into existential questions about how consciousness constructs reality
- **No tolerance:** Like 5-MeO-DMT, 5-MeO-MALT produces no tolerance, allowing repeated dosing without diminishing effects (though Leo warns against abuse)
- **Dosing is critical:** Extremely potent at low doses (5-15mg vaped, 10-50mg plugged depending on sensitivity); starting low and titrating up is essential to avoid overwhelming experiences
- **Psychedelics as primary teaching tool:** Leo argues that 99% of spiritual seekers cannot reach deep god-realization through meditation alone; psychedelics are the most direct and efficient path to understanding the nature of reality, consciousness, and god
- **Solipsistic revelation:** The substance strips away all illusions and false beliefs about enlightenment, teachers, and other people—revealing that everything is infinite imagination and that you are constructing all of reality
- **Research chemical risks:** 5-MeO-MALT is new with few trip reports; potential for unknown side effects, impurities (evidenced by dark coloring), and lack of scientific data on effects across populations
- **Leo's position on spiritual work:** Traditional meditation and self-inquiry are insufficient for most people; psychedelics can accomplish in hours what might take decades of meditation, if it's achievable at all through that method
- **Collective responsibility:** Users have a duty to use these substances responsibly and respectfully, as irresponsible use damages the reputation and availability of psychedelics for future generations who could benefit therapeutically

## Core Concepts

**5-MeO-MALT (5-Methoxymethylamino-1-Phenyl-1-Propanone)**
A novel psychedelic research chemical structurally similar to 5-MeO-DMT but with distinct phenomenological characteristics; produces powerful non-dual consciousness experiences with mild visuals and enhanced stability for introspection.

**Holism and Stability Metric**
Leo's framework for comparing psychedelics based on how much they allow extended, multi-threaded thought chains and sustained focus on existential questions. High holism/stability allows deep introspection into how consciousness constructs reality; low stability leads to loopy, tangential thinking.

**God-Realization**
Direct experiential understanding that consciousness is infinite, non-dual, omnipresent, and that individual identity is a construction within universal consciousness; the recognition that one's mind is dreaming up all of reality including science, logic, other people, and history.

**Solipsism**
The philosophical position that only one's own mind is sure to exist; in Leo's usage, the direct realization that you are constructing all of reality and that everything outside your consciousness is your imagination.

**Psychedelic vs. Meditative Awakening**
Leo's distinction that while both pathways can lead to similar insights, psychedelics achieve in hours what meditation might take 10-20 years to accomplish (if at all), making them the more efficient tool for the 99% of seekers.

**Body Load**
The physical sensations and discomfort experienced during a psychedelic trip; 5-MeO-MALT reportedly has a lower, more manageable body load than 5-MeO-DMT, reducing nausea and physical tension.

**Administration Methods**
Plugging (rectal) vs. vaping; Leo recommends plugging as safer and more controllable, with longer duration (up to 2 hours) compared to vaping (20-30 minutes). Plugging allows better dose control and reduces overdose risk.

## Practical Takeaways

- **Start with extremely low doses:** Begin at 5mg if vaping or 10mg if plugging, regardless of prior psychedelic experience, as 5-MeO-MALT is extraordinarily potent; gradually titrate upward only after assessing tolerance
- **Use plugging over vaping:** Plug (rectally) the substance rather than vaping to avoid overdosing, reduce respiratory harm, and achieve more stable, longer-lasting experiences (1-2 hours vs. 20-30 minutes)
- **Prepare mentally and physically:** Approach this substance only with serious spiritual intent, proper set and setting, and deep introspective motivation; this is not recreational and will confront you with radical truths that destabilize beliefs
- **Check substance purity:** Assess color (whiter is purer), and if the substance appears dark brown (suggesting impurities), dose even lower initially and monitor for adverse reactions
- **Maintain responsible use frequency:** Limit use to no more than once weekly; avoid multiple trips in consecutive days to prevent addiction, mental destabilization, and loss of grounding in daily life
- **Report experiences to community:** Share detailed trip reports on the Actualized.org forum to contribute to collective data-gathering on this novel substance and help others understand its effects and potential
- **Consider legal travel alternatives:** If 5-MeO-MALT is illegal in your jurisdiction, research countries where it is legal and consider traveling for an intentional experience rather than settling for less direct spiritual methods
