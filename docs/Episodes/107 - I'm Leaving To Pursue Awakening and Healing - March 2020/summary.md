## Overview

Leo announces he is taking an extended hiatus from regular content to pursue deep spiritual work following what he describes as his most profound awakening experience yet. He discusses how this awakening transcends all prior teachings and spiritual traditions, reveals insights about the nature of consciousness and healing, and explains his future teaching direction with emphasis on psychedelics as the primary tool for achieving genuine God-realization.

## Key Insights

- **Infinite Depth of Awakening**: Each awakening feels "total and complete" yet reveals deeper layers beneath it. There is no final endpoint to awakening—it's an infinite process of progressively understanding absolute consciousness, with no contradictions between levels, only differences in depth.

- **The Inadequacy of Traditional Practices**: Meditation, yoga, and self-inquiry are "woefully inadequate" for reaching the deepest levels of consciousness Leo is describing. Only psychedelics (particularly 5-MeO-DMT) provide sufficient leverage to access these states in a reasonable timeframe.

- **Disease as Awakening Call**: All suffering, illness, and problems are not punishments but breadcrumbs left by your infinite self to guide you toward awakening. Disease and hardship serve as wake-up calls pushing consciousness toward its own realization.

- **Consciousness Creates Everything**: In this awakening state, Leo became aware that he is consciously creating the entire universe in real-time, moment by moment. The universe is not external—it is his own imagination made manifest, yet he remains largely unconscious of this creative act.

- **Ego as the Central Obstacle**: All human problems (money, relationships, health, meaning) stem from an unconscious, closed-minded, arrogant inner child-self that resists fundamental transformation. Healing requires the "death" of this false self-structure.

- **Love as the Ground of Reality**: True God-realization involves understanding that you are infinite, selfless love. Fear dissolves only through total self-awareness and loving all aspects of yourself. Healing and miracles become possible only through absolute selflessness, never ego.

- **Teaching Will Become More Confrontational**: Leo will become less tolerant of closed-mindedness, games, and ignorance. Future teachings will be more advanced, less appealing to mainstream audiences, and more serious about the radical claim that people are God.

- **Healing Work Requires Integration**: Awakenings disrupt normal life functioning and require extended periods of solitude and embodiment work. Leo's health issues (chronic fatigue, thyroid problems) are not accidents but integral to his spiritual path and his ability to teach healing authentically.

## Core Concepts

**God-Realization**: The ultimate awakening in which you realize you are not separate from God—you ARE God without even an avatar structure. You are infinite, eternal consciousness creating all of reality as your own dream.

**States of Consciousness**: Everything that exists is a state of consciousness. There are no objects, only different degrees and qualities of conscious awareness. Transforming your state transforms your entire reality.

**Self-Transparency**: The awakened state involves consciousness becoming completely transparent to itself—aware of how it is constructing reality in real-time. This transparency reveals you are creating everything.

**Infinite Selflessness**: True God-realization involves infinite selflessness. You love yourself (meaning all of existence) so completely that you created an infinite universe as an expression of that love. Ego desires contradict this state.

**Consciousness as the Only Substance**: There is nothing but consciousness. All apparent matter, separation, and objects are manifestations of consciousness. Materialism and physicalism are fundamental errors in perception.

**Awakening as Obstacle Removal**: Spiritual work is not about gaining something new but removing obstacles (conditioning, beliefs, fears, defense mechanisms, biases) that prevent recognition of your true nature as God.

**Psychedelics as Technology**: 5-MeO-DMT specifically functions as a technology for achieving rapid, radical shifts in consciousness that would take decades through traditional practices (if achievable at all).

## Practical Takeaways

- **Commit to Deep Inner Work**: Real transformation requires willingness to change yourself at fundamental levels—not just peripheral tweaks. Expect this to be far more challenging than anticipated but infinitely more rewarding than imagined.

- **Watch and Rewatch Existing Content**: Leo's catalog contains hundreds of videos dense with wisdom. Watch multiple times, take notes, and allow several years for the material to integrate. This is not casual YouTube content.

- **Investigate Your Obstacles Systematically**: Identify what prevents you from recognizing you are God—whether religious conditioning, atheistic/materialist paradigms, fear, relationship patterns, financial anxieties, health issues, or closed-mindedness—and work to dissolve these.

- **Pursue Radical Open-Mindedness**: Remain willing to question all your assumptions about reality, God, consciousness, science, and yourself. All resistance to new perspectives is ego defending its current structure.

- **Use Suffering as Information**: Rather than trying to eliminate pain and problems, treat them as signals from your infinite self guiding you toward awakening. Ask what each obstacle is trying to teach you.

- **Prepare for Life Disruption**: Major awakenings and serious spiritual work will disrupt normal routines, plans, and social patterns. Accept that periods of instability and confusion are necessary parts of the transformation process, similar to a lobster shedding its shell.

- **Follow the Breadcrumb Trail**: Recognize that every person, book, video, link, and experience in your life has been a breadcrumb trail left by your own consciousness. The more conscious you become, the more you'll recognize this interconnected guidance system.
