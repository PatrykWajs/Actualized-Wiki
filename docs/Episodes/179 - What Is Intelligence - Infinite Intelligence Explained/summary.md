## Core Concepts

**Infinite Intelligence**: A formless, omnipresent, all-powerful field of pure intelligence that is the source and organizing principle of all creation. It is not localized, not mechanical, and transcends logical laws because it designed them.

**Nous (Νοῦς)**: Ancient Greek philosophical term for the divine intellect or faculty of understanding what is true and real. Anaxagoras (510-428 BC) described it as infinite, self-ruled, unmixed, and possessing all knowledge and power—matching Leo's description of infinite intelligence.

**Active Intellect**: Aristotelian concept referring to the universal intelligence that can be conjoined with through direct experience, as described by Islamic philosophers like Averroes and Avicenna.

**Non-dual Enlightenment**: The realization that you are not separate from infinite intelligence but are infinite intelligence itself, temporarily experiencing limitation through human form.

**The Great Strange Loop**: Infinite intelligence's self-creating mechanism—it is so intelligent that it figured out how to go back in time and create itself, the ultimate recursive design.

**Cosmic Causation**: Every object has infinite causes and effects acting upon it simultaneously across infinite dimensions. Science attempts linear causal explanation but misses the infinite-dimensional nature of reality.

**Philosophical Nirvana**: The state of conjoining with infinite intelligence and directly experiencing the structure and perfection of all creation—a metaphysical orgasm of consciousness.
