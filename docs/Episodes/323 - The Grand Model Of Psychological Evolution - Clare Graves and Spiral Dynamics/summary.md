## Core Concepts

**Psychological Evolution** - The model of how human consciousness and psychological structures develop and mature over time, both in individuals and in societies, following predictable stages and patterns.

**Spiral Dynamics** - A framework developed by Beck and Cowan based on Clare Graves' research that maps the stages of consciousness evolution and their characteristics.

**Clare Graves' Model** - A comprehensive theory of how the human psyche develops through distinct stages, each with its own worldview, values, and capabilities.

**The Psyche** - The totality of psychological aspects of the brain and mind, including all mental functions, drives, values, and ways of relating to reality.

**Self-Actualization in Context** - The process of evolving toward one's fullest potential by understanding where you are on the developmental path and what obstacles lie ahead.
